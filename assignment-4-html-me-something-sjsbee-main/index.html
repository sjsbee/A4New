Crusader Kings III

Crusader Kings III is an management roleplaying game released in 2020. Over the course of the game you play the ruler of an area from cradle to grave, and eventually play the descendants of these character for as long as you can keep your head on your shoulders. Rather than talking about myself, I thought it would be more interesting to make a character page for one of the rulers in my first game.

Achievements

Throughout her rule, Hoodo expanded her territory from Ras Hafun to much more of North Eastern Africa, as can be seen in Sanaag section of the second map.

She achieved 'The Sun and The Stars' according to her religious leader.

At the age of eighty, Hoodo had seven children, approximately
50 grandchildren
, and a sturdy hold on her steadily growing empire. Throughout her rule, she expanded educational pursuits, and focused on curing diseases.
